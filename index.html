Ghanian Name finder

What does this application do?


This application should allow you to find an appropriate Akan name.
It will pick the name according to your gender and the day of the week you were born on.

Name finder

Instructions: Input your date of birth and gender into the form below.

Don't worry about the day of the week. The program automatically finds the appropriate week day, or you can use the calendar to select your birth date.
